Welcome to Sumy

Discover the centuries-old history and cultural heritage of the city. Sumy has a rich history that stretches far into the past. The first mentions of Sumy date back to 1652. Over the centuries, Sumy has been an important trade and cultural center in the territory of modern Ukraine. Historical events, such as liberation struggles, wars, and periods of prosperity, have left their mark on the city's architecture and culture.

The city of Sumy is a place with a rich history, unique nature, and fascinating cultural treasures.
