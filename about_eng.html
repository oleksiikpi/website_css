About us

This site is convenient for exploring the history of our city, which you may not have even suspected. Our small team of enthusiasts developed it to uncover little-known facts that remain unnoticed by ordinary residents who might find our city unremarkable. This is an interactive platform where users can explore interesting historical events, personalities, and fascinating places. The site will feature articles, photos, and videos that allow you to immerse yourself in the city's history. .

We sincerely hope that our site will become a small place for you to dive into and fall in love with the city of Sumy even more. We want to develop our site to make it as comfortable as possible for you. Therefore, if you have suggestions for improvement – here is the email where you can send us a message: historyofsumy@example.com
